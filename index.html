About the Office

Building a Safer,
Better Community

The Municipal Engineering Office is the frontline government unit responsible for regulating construction activities, issuing building and related permits, and ensuring that all structures within the municipality comply with the National Building Code of the Philippines and local ordinances.

We are committed to delivering efficient, transparent, and citizen-centered services that protect public safety and support sustainable development in our community.

ISO Compliant NBC Enforcer Green Building Advocate

Our Mission

To provide prompt, efficient, and impartial regulatory services on building construction, renovation, and demolition activities while protecting the health, safety, and welfare of the public.

Our Vision

A highly competent and professional engineering office that serves as a model of excellence in public service — fostering a safe, sustainable, and well-planned municipality.

Permit Requirements

Select a permit type below to view the complete list of documentary requirements. All documents must be originals or certified true copies unless otherwise noted.

Building Permit Requirements

Applicable for new construction, renovation, addition, alteration, or repair of any structure.

A

Basic Documentary Requirements

  • Duly accomplished Building Permit Application Form (BP Form No. 1)
  • Proof of Ownership (TCT/OCT/Tax Declaration)
  • Lot Plan with vicinity map certified by a Licensed Geodetic Engineer
  • Tax Declaration of the property
  • Latest Real Property Tax Receipt
  • Barangay Clearance for construction
B

Architectural Documents

  • Architectural plans (floor plan, elevations, sections) in 5 sets
  • Site development plan
  • Perspective / rendering (for commercial structures)
  • Design Analysis signed by Licensed Architect
C

Structural/Civil Documents

  • Structural plans and specifications signed by Licensed Civil/Structural Engineer
  • Structural Design Analysis (SDA)
  • Soil Boring Test (for 2-storey and above)
  • Bill of Materials and Cost Estimate
D

Professional Credentials

  • PRC License and PTR of all signing professionals
  • IAPOA / IIEE / PICE membership certificate
  • Duly notarized Construction Contract / Supervision Agreement

Electrical Permit Requirements

Required for all electrical installations, upgrades, rewiring, and service connections.

A

Basic Requirements

  • Duly accomplished Electrical Permit Application Form
  • Proof of property ownership or authorization from owner
  • Copy of approved Building Permit (if applicable)
  • Latest Real Property Tax Receipt
B

Electrical Plans & Documents

  • Electrical plans (layout, single-line diagram, panel schedule) in 5 sets
  • Specifications and load schedule
  • Bill of Materials for electrical works
  • Design Analysis signed by Licensed Electrical Engineer
C

Professional Credentials

  • PRC License (RME/REE) and PTR of signing engineer
  • IIEE Membership certificate (current year)
  • Signed Electrical Permit Application (EPC Form)

Certificate of Occupancy Requirements

Issued upon completion of a structure confirming it is safe and ready for use.

A

Basic Documents

  • Accomplished Certificate of Completion / Occupancy Form
  • Original / photocopy of approved Building Permit
  • Approved Electrical Permit (if applicable)
  • Approved Sanitary / Plumbing Permit
  • Latest Real Property Tax Receipt
B

As-Built Plans & Reports

  • As-built Architectural Plans (signed and sealed)
  • As-built Structural Plans (signed and sealed)
  • Logbook of construction activities
  • Certificate of Final Inspection by Building Inspector
C

Clearances Required

  • Fire Safety Inspection Certificate (BFP)
  • Electrical Final Inspection Report (MERALCO/NAPOCOR)
  • Sanitary Clearance from Health Office
  • Barangay Clearance (no pending complaints)

Sanitary / Plumbing Permit Requirements

Required for plumbing installations, drainage systems, and sanitary facilities.

A

Basic Requirements

  • Accomplished Sanitary / Plumbing Permit Application Form
  • Proof of property ownership or written authorization
  • Copy of approved Building Permit
B

Technical Documents

  • Sanitary / Plumbing plans and specifications (5 sets)
  • Isometric diagram of the plumbing system
  • Bill of Materials for plumbing works
  • Signed by Licensed Sanitary Engineer or Master Plumber

Demolition Permit Requirements

Required prior to the demolition or dismantling of any structure.

A

Basic Requirements

  • Accomplished Demolition Permit Application Form
  • Proof of Ownership (TCT/OCT/Tax Declaration)
  • Latest Real Property Tax Receipt
  • Barangay Clearance
B

Technical Documents

  • Site / Lot Plan showing structure to be demolished
  • Demolition plan/method statement signed by Licensed Civil Engineer
  • DENR Clearance (if asbestos or hazardous materials are present)
  • Proof of debris disposal arrangement
